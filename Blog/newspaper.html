Reappointment of AIG Karachi

Javed Alam Odho has been reappointed as Additional Inspector General (AIG) of Karachi police after a notification was issued by the Chief Secretary Sindh on Thursday, Express News reported.

Quetta Gladiators vs. Karachi Kings

KARACHI, Pakistan (AP) — Sherfane Rutherford hit an unbeaten half-century and earned a five-wicket win for Quetta Gladiators over virus-stricken Karachi Kings in the Pakistan Super League on Thursday. Rutherford smashed 58 not out off 31 balls with six towering sixes and a match-winning boundary off the final ball as Quetta recovered from a middle-order collapse to reach 169-5 for their fourth win in five games. Karachi couldn’t cash in on James Vince (37) and Tim Seifert’s (21) impactful batting in the power play before they got tangled against Quetta spinners and were restricted to 165-8. It was Karachi’s third loss in five games.
